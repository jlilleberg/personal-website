About


Hi, I'm Joseph. I'm a data scientist who is passionate about machine learning, algorithms, and big data. I have a Masters in Computer Science from the prestigous University of California, San Diego. I also have two undergraduate degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science.


I’m constantly exploring machine learning concepts and other data science related topics, currently I'm investing my time into neural networks. I’m am skilled in many areas of data science. Some of my skills include: Python, Multivariable Calculus & Linear Algebra, Statistics, Machine Learning, Data Wrangling, Data Visualization & Communication, and Software Engineering.


I am passionate about the intimacy between data science and creativity which is usually underappreciated, an afterthought. Creativity begets data science so it's important to understand how the two are woven together. Therefore, I strive to produce excellent software, with creativity at the forefront, that improves the lives of those around me or at the very least, inspires their creative ambitions.
