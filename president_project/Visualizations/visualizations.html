Important: Plot Explanations
The following descriptions below explain the types of plots and the information they convey. Please note that some interactive visualizations take longer than others to render.

LDA Topics: Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) is an example of topic model and is used to classify text in a document to a particular topic. It builds a topic per document model and words per topic model, modeled as Dirichlet distributions.

Term Associations: Compares the term associations between two entities

Empaths: Compares Empath topics and categories between two entities

Term Frequency: Compares the frequency of terms between two entities

Topic Frequency: Compares the frequency of topics between two entities

Word Similarity: Compares the word similiarities between two entities
